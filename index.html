Privacy Policy — Settle: Baby Names

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Settle: Baby Names Android app (com.settlebabynames) collects, how it is used, and your choices. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Google account information (via Google Sign-In): your name, email address, profile picture, and Google account identifier. We request only basic profile and email access, and use this to create and authenticate your account.
  • Content you create in the app: your baby-name preferences (the names you like or pass on), your saved name lists, and the link between you and a partner account so the two of you can collaborate.

How we use your information

We use it solely to operate the app: to sign you in, store your preferences, and share names between you and your linked partner. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your data is held on our backend hosted on Google Cloud Platform (Cloud Run) in a managed PostgreSQL database. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Third-party services

We rely on Google for authentication (Google Sign-In) and for cloud hosting (Google Cloud Platform). We do not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Children

Settle is intended for adults (prospective parents) and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change to reflect any revisions.
