Privacy Policy — Settle: Baby Names

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Settle: Baby Names Android app (com.settlebabynames) collects, how it is used, and your choices. By using the app you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information (via Google Sign-In): your email address and your Google account identifier. We use Google Sign-In with basic profile and email access to create and authenticate your account. We do not store your Google name or profile picture.
  • Content and activity you create in the app: the names you approve, reject, or super-like; your filter preferences (gender, era of origin, and cultural origin); any custom names you add yourself; your ranked shortlist of matched names; and the link between your account and your partner's account so the two of you can collaborate.

How we use your information

We use it solely to operate the app: to sign you in, store your preferences, and share matched names between you and your linked partner. We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Where your data is stored

Your data is held on our backend hosted on Google Cloud Platform (Cloud Run) in a managed PostgreSQL database. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections, and your sign-in token is held in your device's secure storage (Android Keystore).

Third-party services

We rely on Google for authentication (Google Sign-In) and for cloud hosting (Google Cloud Platform). We do not use any third-party advertising, analytics, or crash-reporting services, and we do not share your data with third parties.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your data while your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by emailing us at the address below, and we will delete it.

Children

Settle is intended for adults (prospective parents) and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will change to reflect any revisions.
