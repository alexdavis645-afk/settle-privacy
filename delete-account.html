Delete Your Account — Settle: Baby Names

Last updated: 16 June 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your account and associated data in the Settle: Baby Names app (com.settlebabynames). See also our Privacy Policy.

How to request deletion

  1. Email DaveAppDevelopment@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Settle account.
  2. Use the subject line “Delete my account”.
  3. We will verify your request and permanently delete your account and associated data within 30 days, and confirm by email once done.

What is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove all data associated with it:

  • Your account details: email address and Google account identifier
  • Your name preferences and swipe history (names you approved, rejected, or super-liked)
  • Your filter settings (gender, era of origin, cultural origin)
  • Any custom names you added
  • Your ranked shortlist of matched names
  • The link between your account and your partner's account
  • Your active login sessions

What is kept, and for how long

We do not retain your personal data after deletion. Residual copies in routine backups are purged within 30 days of your request. We keep no account data for marketing or analytics, and we do not retain data for any additional period beyond what is needed to complete deletion.
